<div style="max-width: 550px; margin: 2rem auto; font-family: Arial, sans-serif; color: #666666; font-size: 0.875rem;">
  <div style="color: #0000d2; line-height: 1.2; font-weight: bold;">
    Wireless 2DT technology using electromagnetic waves was launched from our lab. <a href="https://drive.google.com/file/d/1SDWjMRIjuIFmbkwQRcDr7J3nPIQcXBMV/view?usp=drive_link" style="color: #0000d2;">(Oct. 2006)</a>
  </div>
  <br/>
  Electromagnetic waves typically in microwave frequency range are confined and guided in a thin sheet-like 2D medium. A coupler put near the surface can be coupled with the medium selectively and efficiently, which enables safe wireless power transmission in general environments and wide-band signal transmission free from interfering with the outside space. The medium can be produced from various low cost materials.
  <br/><br/>
  **The following terms represent the identical technology**<br/>
  Two-dimensional communication (2DC)<br/>
  Two-dimensional signal transmission (2DST) technology<br/>
  Two-dimensional waveguide power transmission (2DWPT)<br/>
  Two-dimensional waveguide transmission (2DWT)<br/>
  <br/>
  **The followings are also frequently used**<br/>
  Surface signal/power supply, Surface communication, Surface LAN, Surface network<br/>
  <br/>
  **Current commercial products using 2DT technology**<br/>
  LAN Sheet (Itoki Corp.), Cell Form (Teijin Ltd.), Touch LAN (Icom Inc.), Ultra-Thin RFID Antenna (Convergence Systems Limited), @Cell LAN, @Cell RFID (Cellcross Co., Ltd.), etc..
</div>
